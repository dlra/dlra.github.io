London Parks

What I love about London is it parks. There are so many, and they are quite varied.

Burgess Park

Burgess Park was built between the 1950s and 1980s. It came about as a result of the Abecrombie Plan in 1943, which set out ideas for open spaces in London. It is still a 'live' park, inasmuch as it is unfinished in its development.
